AIC turns official historical Chicago incident records into a quick, descriptive Cooked Score Beta.

Tap Scan Me or choose a point on the offline Chicago picker. AIC estimates the concentration of four supported incident categories for a fixed 500-metre circle and compares the result with eligible Chicago reference locations. The scan runs on your iPhone against a bundled data pack.

AT A GLANCE
• Cooked Score Beta, rounded to the nearest five
• Relative Chicago percentile and estimated contributing incident count
• Assault/battery, robbery, theft, and motor-vehicle theft breakdown
• Approximate neighborhood and source-through date
• On-device Cooked Receipt with username and neighborhood controls
• Native iOS sharing with no precise location in the receipt

BUILT FOR LOCATION PRIVACY
AIC does not send normal scan coordinates, manual pins, addresses, routes, geographic cells, or scan history to its account service. It never requests background location. If you deny location access, the manual Chicago picker remains available.

IMPORTANT LIMITATION
Cooked Score Beta compares historical reported-incident concentration around this location with eligible Chicago comparison locations. It is not a live safety assessment or personal-risk prediction. It does not promise that a place is safe or unsafe and should not be used for emergency or navigation decisions.

Chicago only during this beta. Source coverage and methodology are shown in the app.
