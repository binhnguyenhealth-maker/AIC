# City of Chicago terms evidence — 2026-07-12

## Scope

This dated record supports AIC's use of the City of Chicago datasets identified
as `ijzp-q8t2`, `c7ck-438e`, and `igwz-8jzy`. It is release evidence, not legal
advice or a substitute for qualified counsel.

## Primary-source checks

- The official [City of Chicago Data Terms of Use and Website
  Disclaimer](https://www.chicago.gov/city/en/narr/foia/data_disclaimer.html)
  requires secondary or derivative applications to display the prescribed City
  notice at the place where the app can be accessed or downloaded.
- The official [Crimes — 2001 to Present metadata
  record](https://data.cityofchicago.org/api/views/ijzp-q8t2) identifies the
  dataset as official, attributes it to the Chicago Police Department, labels its
  license `SEE_TERMS_OF_USE`, describes daily updates, and states the source's
  accuracy, revision, approximation, and comparison limitations.
- The official metadata records for [IUCR
  Codes](https://data.cityofchicago.org/api/views/c7ck-438e) and [Community Area
  Boundaries](https://data.cityofchicago.org/api/views/igwz-8jzy) were also
  checked as the two supporting release sources.

## Release conformance checked

- The exact City-prescribed derivative-application notice is retained in
  `DATA_SOURCES.md` and appears in the App Store description and live Terms page.
- AIC identifies the three datasets and does not use City or Chicago Police
  branding in a way that implies endorsement.
- AIC discloses that data is historical, preliminary, approximate, revisable,
  incomplete, and unsuitable for emergency, navigation, or personal-safety
  decisions.
- The released pack contains aggregates and omits incident IDs, addresses,
  coordinates, dates, case numbers, and exact cell totals.

## Remaining legal-risk boundary

No attorney has reviewed this release. The City terms include continuing
obligations and risk allocation beyond displaying the notice. The founder must
make the App Store Content Rights attestation and should obtain qualified legal
review if launch-risk tolerance requires counsel rather than a primary-source
conformance check.
