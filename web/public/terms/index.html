Cooked Score is a historical data index that compares reported-incident concentration around this location with eligible Chicago comparison locations. It is not a live safety assessment or personal-risk prediction. Do not use AIC to make emergency, navigation, housing, employment, insurance, lending, policing, or other high-impact decisions. Historical public data

Results depend on preliminary public records that may be delayed, incomplete, revised, misclassified, or geographically approximate. Reported incidents do not include unreported events and do not establish causality. A score can change when the source or method changes.
