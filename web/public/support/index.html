Beta support

AIC is currently distributed as a limited Chicago TestFlight beta.

Send feedback or report a problem

  1. Open the TestFlight app.
  2. Select AIC.
  3. Tap Send Beta Feedback.
  4. Include what you expected, what happened, and your iOS version. Do not include an exact location.

Location permission denied

Use Choose on Chicago map in AIC for the offline manual-pin flow. AIC does not require background location.

Delete your account

In AIC, open account settings, choose Delete account, review the warning, reauthenticate with Apple, and confirm. See the account-deletion page for the complete flow.

Score limitations

Cooked Score Beta compares historical reported-incident concentration around this location with eligible Chicago comparison locations. It is not a live safety assessment or personal-risk prediction. For an emergency, contact local emergency services.
