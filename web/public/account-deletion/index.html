Accounts in AIC v1

AIC v1 has no account, sign-in, or public-username feature. It does not create or access account records, so there is no AIC account data to delete.

Local app data

Deleting the app removes its local preferences and temporary receipts. It cannot remove a receipt you saved to Photos, sent through another app, or that another person copied or screenshotted.
